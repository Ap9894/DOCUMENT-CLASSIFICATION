Ronaldinho is Back in the National Team – Faces England


		 			socrates / 22 January, 2013

	 	 		 0 Flares 0 Flares ×

Brazil face England at Wembley in February. Ronadinho has been selected for the squad.

This means that Ronaldinho is back, the former Barcelona star now play in Atletico Mineiro is one of six players from the Brazilian league that is selected for the squad against England.

The match is the part of the FA’s 150th anniversary and played February 6.

Here Brazil’s squad for the England game:

Julio Cesar (QPR), Diego Alves (Valencia), Daniel Alves, Adriano (both Barcelona), Luiz (Chelsea), Dante (Bayern Munich), Leandro Castan (Roma), Miranda, Filipe Luis (both Atletico Madrid), Ramires (Chelsea), Arouca (Santos), Paulinho (Corinthians), Hernanes (Lazio), Oscar (Chelsea), Ronaldinho (Atletico Mineiro), Moura (PSG), Hulk (Zenit St Petersburg), Neymar (Santos), Fred (Fluminense) , Luis Fabiano (Sao Paulo).
